Steamed Broccoli

Description

A recipe for cheesy and tangy steamed broccoli

Ingredients

  • Frozen Broccoli
  • Garlic Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Romano Cheese
  • Balsamic Vinaigrette

Steps

  1. Place frozen broccoli in a steaming dish
  2. Cook for 2 minutes at a time until broccoli is hot and soft to the touch
  3. Place steamed broccoli in a dry bowl
  4. Add garlic salt, parmesan cheese, and romano cheese
  5. Add slight amount of balsamic vinaigrette
  6. Stir until broccoli is evenly coated in spices and cheeses
  7. Taste and add more balsamic vinaigrette as needed
