Ribeye Steak

Description

A recipe for juicy, salt and pepper seasoned ribeye steak

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Set the steak out until it reaches room temperature
  2. Generously coat both sides of the steak with salt and pepper
  3. Heat grill to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Cook 4 minutes per side for medium rare steak
  5. Cook 5 minutes per side for medium steak
  6. Cook 6 minutes per side for well done steak
